# Chucky's Bar — Brand Guidelines

## Business Info
- **Name:** Chucky's Bar (stylized: CHUCKYS BAR)
- **Type:** Sports bar, beach bar, family venue, nightlife
- **Address:** Unit 3 & 4, Phoenix Center, Malibongwe Dr, Ferndale, Randburg, 2194
- **Phone:** 082 666 0319
- **Price Range:** R200–300 per person
- **Rating:** 4.7 stars (89 Google reviews), 5/5 Facebook (7 votes)

## What They Offer
- Sports bar with big screens
- Dance area
- DJs every Friday & Saturday
- Live music & cocktails on Sundays
- Outdoor seating
- Great cocktails (Mojito, Vodka Soda, Stars & Stripes, etc.)
- Events: Comedy nights, magic shows, groove parties
- Food: Bunny chow and bar food

## Brand Colors (extracted from signage)
- **Primary Red:** #E31E24 (the "CHUCKYS" text)
- **White:** #FFFFFF (the "BAR" text and accents)
- **Electric Blue:** #0066FF (building exterior, accent)
- **Vibrant Green:** #00AA44 (from the colorful rays/foliage)
- **Dark/Black:** #1A1A1A (backgrounds)
- **Accent colors from rays:** red, green, blue, yellow — bold, carnival-like palette

## Typography
- Signage uses a bold, blocky Western/slab-serif style for "CHUCKYS"
- "BAR" in a cleaner condensed sans-serif with decorative dashes
- Overall feel: bold, fun, high-energy

## Brand Personality
- Fun, loud, vibrant, welcoming
- Party atmosphere but also family-friendly
- South African township bar energy meets modern sports bar
- Unpretentious, affordable, good vibes

## Venue Vibe
- Colorful exterior (blue walls, red roof trim)
- Indoor-outdoor feel
- Neon/LED accents
- Event-driven (regular weekly programming)
